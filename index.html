It has been claimed Paris Saint-Germain superstar Lionel Messi will return to Barcelona at the end of the season. The 34-year-old joined the Catalan club in August last year after his contract in Spain expired and Barca decided that renewing his deal would be financially impossible.
Since his move to the French giants, while there have been flashes of brilliance, Messi has been subjected to a lot of criticism for his overall impact on the side.
This has seemingly led the son of legendary Argentine footballer Diego Maradona, Diego Sinagra, to believe the seven-time Ballon d'Or winner will make his way back to his former club once this campaign is concluded.
Messi has scored seven goals and assisted a further nine strikes in 22 games in all competitions for PSG so far this season, and has missed eight matches due to injury.
